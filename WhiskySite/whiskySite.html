Welcome to WhiskyRater

Welcome!

This document was started by a few friends looking to bounce tasting notes off each other to see what needs to be tried, and what can be skipped out on completely. Please take the time to follow the formatting, and post your reviews below! If you're writing your first review, please create your own section with your name, or some unique alias.

Whisky Rating Scale:

  • 0: Horrible, worst thing I've ever drank
  • 1: If I was already drunk, I MIGHT think about drinking this
  • 2: Might be okay if drowned with soda or juice
  • 3: Still not great neat, but with some ice or chase its drinkable
  • 4: If given neat could drink it, but worse than the average (higher end) scotch
  • 5: The mean, drinkable neat but nothing special
  • 6: Above average, quite nice, but once again nothing incredible
  • 7: Nice whisky, something I would consider buying
  • 8: Great whisky, definitely something I would buy if available/affordable
  • 9: Incredible whisky, one of my all time favourites, I would buy it even at secondary pricing
  • 10: Unicorn, nothing should ever reach this level
